subroutine am_adjust_calc_permfield()

expon = thole_expon_coeff * delr2 * delr * sq_polinv(i) * sq_polinv(j)

sq_polinv() is atm_qterm array of prmtop_dat_mod   in calling subroutine am_adjust_permfield()
